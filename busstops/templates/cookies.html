Privacy

You can use most of this website freely without sharing any personal details.

Using the “edit vehicle information” feature requires an email address, to facilitate logging in, and so that I may email you if necessary. If you create an account and log in, I may also record your IP address, to help identify and prevent abuse and vandalism.

If you email me, I ask for your name and email address, in order that I can reply. I may forward your message to e.g. a bus operating company or transport authority, if they are better placed to help you, unless you ask me not to.

This website embeds some content from third parties: map tiles from Stadia Maps, analytics code from Google Analytics, and programmatic advertisements under the auspices of AdFirst Media.

Cookies

‘Cookies’ are small text files stored on your computer when you use some websites, including this one.

We use some cookies for the functionality of this website:

Name Type Purpose
vehicleMap local storage Last viewed location on the map
hideStops local storage Whether to show bus stops on the map
sessionid cookie Remember if you have logged in

We use Google Analytics to measure website usage, which sets these cookies:

Name Default expiration time Purpose
_ga 2 years Distinguish users
_ga_<container-id> 2 years Persist session state
